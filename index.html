Legend Of Houdini

In his will, Houdini left strict instructions that all his most closely guarded secrets and artefacts should be "burned and destroyed" by his surviving brother, Theodore Hardeen. Fortunately for us, this never came to pass, as brother Theo decided to sell them to a private collector.

Along with his famous stage props, Houdini's legacy includes one unique item that points to his true genius as a card magician and illusionist.

For decades, this photograph lay gathering dust in a box alongside the many other surviving publicity shots of the artist... until quite recently, when the original photographic plates were digitally scanned for the very first time...
